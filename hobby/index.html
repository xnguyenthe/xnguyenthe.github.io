Piano playing

Playing the piano is among my favourite activities to, as they say in German, "chase away the time" with. I've been playing the, to the eye rather ordinary looking, instrument of piano since the dawning years of my life. Did that sound too pretentious? I mean since around 4 :) My preferred musical movement is Impressionism, within which I find myself most inclined to Mr. Claude Debussy and his dreamy, light and liberating style.
